## 2. Skill Package Pricing Analysis

### Top 3 Package Examples

#### A. Smart Content Writer - $19/month
**Value Proposition:** Multilingual content creation with SEO optimization
- Target: Content marketers, bloggers, SEO agencies
- Pricing: Competitive with tools like Jasper.ai ($39/month), Copy.ai ($49/month)
- Malaysian Market Edge: BM/EN bilingual support, local SEO integration

#### B. WhatsApp Support Agent - $49/month  
**Value Proposition:** 24/7 customer support via WhatsApp Business API
- Target: Malaysian SMEs (restaurants, clinics, retail)
- Pricing: 60% cheaper than human support staff
- Malaysian Market Edge: PDPA compliance, local business number integration

#### C. Loan Comparison Agent - Free (Revenue via leads)
**Value Proposition:** Personal loan comparison engine
- Target: Loan seekers, financial advisors
- Revenue: CPL (Cost Per Lead) model - RM 50-80 per qualified lead
- Malaysian Market Edge: All local banks, Islamic loan support

## 4. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Weeks 1-4)
- **Free listings** for all early adopters
- **Featured listings** at $9/month (50% off)
- **Onboarding bonus**: First 100 creators get free Pro plan for 3 months

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 2-3)
- Introduce **Featured** ($19) and **Professional** ($99) tiers
- **Launch Product Hunt** with top 3 skill packages
- **Partnership program** with Malaysian tech incubators

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 4-6)
- **Enterprise tier** ($499) for large customers
- **API access** for SaaS integration
- **International expansion** to Singapore and Indonesia
