## 📝 Product Description

**AgentHub** is Southeast Asia's first dedicated marketplace for discovering, sharing, and selling AI-powered skill packages. Whether you're a developer building custom agents, an agency creating specialized tools, or a business seeking AI solutions, AgentHub connects creators with customers who need their expertise.

### What we solve:
- **For Creators:** Turn your AI skills into recurring revenue with ready-to-skill packages
- **For Businesses:** Access affordable, specialized AI tools without building from scratch  
- **For Developers:** Share and monetize your AI agents through a trusted marketplace

### Key Features:
- 🚀 **Skill Package Marketplace**: Browse 100+ AI agents for marketing, support, finance, and more
- 💰 **Instant Monetization**: Set your price, collect payments, get paid 70% of revenue
- 🔍 **Smart Discovery**: Advanced search by category, pricing, and use case
- ⚡ **One-Click Installation**: Deploy any skill package in minutes
- 🌏 **Bilingual Support**: Built for Malaysian market with BM/EN interfaces
