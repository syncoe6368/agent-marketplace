## 1. Revenue Model Clarification

### A. Primary Model: Platform Discovery (80% of Value)
**What we sell:** Agent discovery + skill package distribution  
**User pays:** To get their agent in front of buyers  
**Creator pays:** Listing fees to gain visibility  
**Buyer pays:** 0 (free discovery, pay for specific packages)

**Core Value:** We are a **discovery platform**, not a hosting platform.  
This massively reduces complexity vs. traditional SaaS models.

### B. Secondary Model: Value-Added Services (20% of Value)
**What we sell:** Tools to help creators succeed  
**Examples:** Analytics, API access, verified badges, custom branding  
**Higher margin services** that build on platform success
