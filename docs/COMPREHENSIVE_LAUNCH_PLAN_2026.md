### 6. Risk Mitigation

#### Launch Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Low Creator Adoption**
- **Mitigation:** Strong onboarding, clear documentation, success stories
- **Monitoring:** Creator signups, agent submissions, feature adoption

**Risk 2: Technical Issues at Launch**
- **Mitigation:** Load testing, rollback plan, support team on standby
- **Monitoring:** Uptime, response times, error rates

**Risk 3: Insufficient Initial Content**
- **Mitigation:** Pre-populate with 8+ quality agents, clear publishing guidelines
- **Monitoring:** Content quality scores, user engagement rates

**Risk 4: Competitive Response**
- **Mitigation:** Focus on Southeast Asian advantage, local language support
- **Monitoring:** Competitor feature launches, market positioning
