# Agent Marketplace Creator Outreach Campaign

**Purpose:** Drive initial creator adoption and build a vibrant marketplace ecosystem  
**Timeline:** 2-week campaign starting immediately  
**Target:** 50+ active creators by launch  
**Status:** Ready for execution

---

## Campaign Overview

### Why This Matters
- **Creator density** drives buyer adoption
- **Early adopters** get preferential treatment and visibility
- **Network effects** compound exponentially as more agents join
- **Social proof** is critical for marketplace validation

### Campaign Goals
- **50 creators** signed up in first 2 weeks
- **200+ skill packages** listed during launch
- **15 featured agents** driving discovery
- **80% retention** at 30 days

## Risk Mitigation

### Common Objections & Responses
**"Not sure if my agents will sell"**
> "We provide free featured placement during launch and our discovery platform drives organic traffic. Many creators see their first sales within days."

**"Too much competition"**
> "We're focused on the Southeast Asian market with local language support, which gives you a competitive edge. Plus, featured placement gives you visibility."

**"Don't understand the technical setup"**
> "It's incredibly simple - just link to your existing API endpoint. We handle all the discovery, payment, and user management."

### Campaign Risks
- **Low Response Rate:** Scale up community outreach and partnerships
- **Quality Concerns:** Provide creator onboarding and support
- **Technical Issues:** Offer setup support and documentation
